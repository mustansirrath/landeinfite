About this course

This all-inclusive course is designed to take you on a journey through U.S. accounting principles and practices, from foundational concepts to advanced techniques. Whether you’re a beginner seeking to enter the world of accounting or a professional aiming to elevate your skills, this training provides the knowledge and hands-on experience needed to excel.

With a focus on practical applications, you’ll master essential accounting tasks using QuickBooks Online (QBO) and QuickBooks Desktop (QBD), equipping you to handle real-world accounting challenges confidently and efficiently.



Topics Covered:

1) Introduction to Accounting

2) Recording Transactions

3) Bank Reconciliations

4) Payroll Management

5) Accounts Payable and Receivable

6) Inventory Tracking

7) Budgeting and Forecasting

8) Financial Analysis

9) Multi-Currency Accounting

10) Tax Preparation

What You’ll Learn:

- Fundamental accounting principles and how to apply them in practice

- Intermediate skills like reconciliation, payroll, and inventory management

- Advanced techniques for budgeting, forecasting, and financial analysis

- Proficiency in QuickBooks Online and Desktop for a wide range of accounting tasks


Why Take This Course?

This training equips you with a complete accounting toolkit, enabling you to progress from basic bookkeeping to handling complex accounting tasks. Whether you’re preparing for a career in accounting or managing your own business finances, this course ensures you have the expertise needed to succeed.


Who Should Take This Course?

- Aspiring Accountants and Bookkeepers

- Accounting Professionals Looking to Enhance Their Skills

- Entrepreneurs and Small Business Owners

- Finance Professionals Seeking Proficiency in QuickBooks

Course Content

Unit 1: Fundamentals of Accounting

Understand the core principles and concepts of accounting essential for advanced practices.

Unit 2: Introduction to U.S. Accounting with QuickBooks Online (QBO)

Gain an overview of U.S. accounting practices and learn the basics of navigating QuickBooks Online.

Unit 3: Methods of Accounting

Explore cash and accrual basis accounting and understand their application in real-world scenarios.

Unit 4: Transaction Posting in QuickBooks Online

Learn the step-by-step process for recording transactions accurately in QBO.

Unit 5: Reconciliation of Accounts

Master the techniques for reconciling bank accounts, credit cards, and other financial accounts.

Unit 6: Recording of Assets and Loans

Understand how to record and manage fixed assets, liabilities, and loan accounts effectively.

Unit 7: Understanding the Use of Key Accounts

Deepen your knowledge of key account types and their roles in financial reporting and management.

Unit 8: Finalize Books

Learn to finalize books for accuracy, ensuring compliance and readiness for reporting.

Unit 9: Practice Accounts

Apply your skills through hands-on practice with real-world accounting scenarios.

Unit 10: Requirements & Process of Review of Books

Learn the essential requirements and step-by-step process for a thorough book review.

Unit 11: Review & Resolution of Reconciliation Discrepancy

Master techniques to identify and resolve reconciliation discrepancies effectively.

Unit 12: Intercompany & CPLTD

Understand intercompany transactions and the management of Current Portion of Long-Term Debt (CPLTD).

Unit 13: General Review of Accounts

Develop skills to conduct a comprehensive review of all accounts for accuracy and completeness.

Unit 14: Review and Adjustments of Key Accounts

Learn how to review and adjust key accounts like A/R, A/P, and Payroll for accuracy.

Unit 15: Communication of Issues to Client and Finalization

Master the art of communicating accounting issues and finalizing financial statements with clients.

Unit 16: Report Analysis & Highlighting Key Ratios

Analyze financial reports and highlight critical ratios for informed decision-making.

Unit 17: Practice Accounts

Apply learned concepts through practical exercises using real-world accounts in QuickBooks.
