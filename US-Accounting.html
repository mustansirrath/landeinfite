About this course

This course is your comprehensive guide to mastering the essentials of bookkeeping for businesses in the U.S. Whether you're new to bookkeeping or looking to sharpen your skills, this course will provide you with the knowledge and practical experience needed to efficiently manage financial records for businesses of all sizes.

You’ll learn to navigate both QuickBooks Online and QuickBooks Desktop for bookkeeping on both a cash and accrual basis. From posting transactions to reconciling bank accounts, you’ll gain hands-on experience in handling the full range of bookkeeping tasks. By the end of this course, you’ll feel confident in your ability to manage a business's books accurately and efficiently.



Topics Covered:

1) Introduction to Bookkeeping

2) Navigating QuickBooks Online and Desktop

3) Cash vs. Accrual Basis Bookkeeping

4) Posting Transactions and Recording Entries

5) Bank and Credit Card Reconciliation

6) Managing Accounts Receivable and Payable

7) Finalizing Books and Reporting

8) Practical Bookkeeping for Business

What You’ll Learn:

- How to use QuickBooks Online and Desktop for cash and accrual basis bookkeeping

- The complete process of managing business transactions from start to finish

- How to reconcile accounts and generate accurate financial reports

- Practical skills that can be applied to any business, from startups to established enterprises


Why Learn Bookkeeping?

Bookkeeping is an essential skill for anyone managing or working in a business. It ensures that financial records are accurate, helping you make informed decisions, comply with regulations, and maintain financial health. Mastering bookkeeping gives you the foundation to become a skilled accounting professional or better manage your own business finances.


Who Should Take This Course?

- Aspiring Bookkeepers and Accountants

- Small Business Owners and Entrepreneurs

- Professionals Looking to Enhance Their Financial Skills

- Anyone Interested in Pursuing a Career in Accounting or Finance


Prerequisites

No prior bookkeeping or accounting experience is required. This course is designed to be beginner-friendly, offering a clear path from foundational concepts to practical bookkeeping tasks.

Course Content

Unit 1: Fundamentals of Accounting

Understand the core principles and concepts of accounting essential for advanced practices.

Unit 2: Introduction to U.S. Accounting with QuickBooks Online (QBO)

Gain an overview of U.S. accounting practices and learn the basics of navigating QuickBooks Online.

Unit 3: Methods of Accounting

Explore cash and accrual basis accounting and understand their application in real-world scenarios.

Unit 4: Transaction Posting in QuickBooks Online

Learn the step-by-step process for recording transactions accurately in QBO.

Unit 5: Reconciliation of Accounts

Master the techniques for reconciling bank accounts, credit cards, and other financial accounts.

Unit 6: Recording of Assets and Loans

Understand how to record and manage fixed assets, liabilities, and loan accounts effectively.

Unit 7: Understanding the Use of Key Accounts

Deepen your knowledge of key account types and their roles in financial reporting and management.

Unit 8: Finalize Books

Learn to finalize books for accuracy, ensuring compliance and readiness for reporting.

Unit 9: Practice Accounts

Apply your skills through hands-on practice with real-world accounting scenarios.
