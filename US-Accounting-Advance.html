About this course

This advanced course is designed for professionals seeking to deepen their expertise in U.S. business accounting. Perfect for seasoned accountants, bookkeepers, and finance professionals, the training focuses on mastering advanced accounting processes and leveraging both QuickBooks Online and Desktop to handle complex tasks.

From categorization and payroll posting to intercompany account reconciliation and book finalization, this course equips you with the tools and techniques to review and manage business finances with precision. By the end, you’ll have the confidence to handle sophisticated accounting review tasks and ensure accuracy in financial reporting.



Topics Covered:

1) Advanced Accounting Fundamentals

2) Navigating QuickBooks for Advanced Tasks

3) Categorization and Expense Tracking

4) Payroll Posting and Reconciliation

5) Intercompany Accounts Management

6) Accounting Review Process

7) Book Finalization and Reporting

8) Best Practices in Advanced Accounting

What You’ll Learn:

- How to perform advanced accounting tasks using QuickBooks Online and Desktop

- Techniques for reviewing, analyzing, and finalizing books with precision

- Best practices for managing payroll, intercompany accounts, and financial reports

- Practical skills to tackle complex accounting challenges

Course Content

Unit 1: Requirements & Process of Review of Books

Learn the essential requirements and step-by-step process for a thorough book review.

Unit 2: Review & Resolution of Reconciliation Discrepancy

Master techniques to identify and resolve reconciliation discrepancies effectively.

Unit 3: Intercompany & CPLTD

Understand intercompany transactions and the management of Current Portion of Long-Term Debt (CPLTD).

Unit 4: General Review of Accounts

Develop skills to conduct a comprehensive review of all accounts for accuracy and completeness.

Unit 5: Review and Adjustments of Key Accounts

Learn how to review and adjust key accounts like A/R, A/P, and Payroll for accuracy.

Unit 6: Communication of Issues to Client and Finalization

Master the art of communicating accounting issues and finalizing financial statements with clients.

Unit 7: Report Analysis & Highlighting Key Ratios

Analyze financial reports and highlight critical ratios for informed decision-making.

Unit 8: Practice Accounts

Apply learned concepts through practical exercises using real-world accounts in QuickBooks.
